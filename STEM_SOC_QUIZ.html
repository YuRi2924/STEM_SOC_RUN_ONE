EARTH SCIENCE

Earth Science is the study of the Earth and its processes, encompassing various fields such as geology, meteorology, oceanography, astronomy, and environmental science. It explores the Earth's structure, materials, and the forces that shape its surface, like plate tectonics, volcanoes, and earthquakes. It also examines weather patterns, atmospheric phenomena, and climate, along with the oceans' physical, chemical, and biological characteristics.

GENERAL BIOLOGY

General Biology is the study of living organisms and their interactions with the environment. It covers a wide range of topics, including the structure, function, growth, evolution, and distribution of life forms. By examining cells, genetics, ecosystems, and biodiversity, General Biology provides a comprehensive understanding of the biological processes that sustain life on Earth.

LEARN MORE

PRE-CALCULUS

Pre-Calculus is a mathematical course that prepares students for the study of calculus. It combines concepts from algebra and trigonometry to build a solid foundation for understanding more advanced mathematical topics. This course also explores functions, sequences, and the behavior of graphs, which are essential for solving real-world problems in higher-level mathematics.

LEARN MORE

CALCULUS

Calculus is a branch of mathematics that focuses on the study of change and motion. It is divided into two main areas: differential calculus and integral calculus. These two branches work together to analyze and solve problems related to rates of change, optimization, and the accumulation of quantities, making calculus a fundamental tool in fields such as physics, engineering, and economics.

LEARN MORE

PHYSICS

Physics is the scientific study of matter, energy, and the fundamental forces of nature, aiming to understand how the universe behaves. It uses mathematical models and experimental methods to explain natural phenomena and predict future events, making it essential for technological advancements and scientific discoveries.

LEARN MORE

CHEMISTRY

Chemistry is the branch of science that studies the composition, structure, properties, and reactions of matter. It focuses on the interactions between atoms and molecules and how they combine and transform to create new substances.
