EARTH SCIENCE

Earth Science is the study of the Earth and its processes, encompassing various fields such as geology, meteorology, oceanography, astronomy, and environmental science. It explores the Earth's structure, materials, and the forces that shape its surface, like plate tectonics, volcanoes, and earthquakes. It also examines weather patterns, atmospheric phenomena, and climate, along with the oceans' physical, chemical, and biological characteristics.
