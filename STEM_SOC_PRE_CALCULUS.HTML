PRE-CALCULUS

Pre-calculus is a mathematical course that prepares students for the study of calculus. It combines elements of algebra and trigonometry, focusing on functions, equations, and their graphs. Topics include polynomial, rational, exponential, logarithmic, and trigonometric functions, as well as sequences, series, and limits. Pre-calculus provides the foundation for understanding calculus concepts like rates of change and accumulation.
